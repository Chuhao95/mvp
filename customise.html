Customise Predictor Instructions

  1. Download thumbnail click-through data from your YouTube Studio:
    • Go to Analytics in your YouTube Studio.
    • Click on Advanced.
    • Change the date range to Lifetime.
    • Click on Download.
    • Unzip the downloaded file and locate Table Data.csv.
  2. Send your YouTube's channel name along with the Table Data.csv file to chuhaoliu1995@gmail.com.
  3. Within 24 hours, a link to your customised predictor will be provided in the reply email.
